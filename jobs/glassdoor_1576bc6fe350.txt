 **About the Role**


We are looking for a world\-class Google Ads Specialist. Someone who lives and breathes paid acquisition and is comfortable managing complex accounts, high budgets, and demanding clients.


You must be able to:

* architect advanced campaign structures
* optimize toward real business outcomes
* explain your strategy clearly to clients
* diagnose and fix technical tracking issues
* make confident decisions backed by data
* operate with senior\-level ownership


This is **not a junior role.**


This is **not a “set it and forget it”** role.


We are looking for a Google Ads genius. Someone who knows their craft at a very high level and can drive meaningful performance improvements.


You will work mainly with e\-commerce and professional service brands across North America. You will collaborate with our internal creative, CRO, SEO, and dev teams to produce full\-funnel marketing results.

 **Key Responsibilities**


Google Ads Strategy \& Execution

* Plan, build, and optimize advanced Google Ads campaigns (Search, Performance Max, Display, YouTube, remarketing, Shopping, Demand Gen).
* Develop smart bidding strategies based on business objectives, CAC targets, ROAS goals, and revenue metrics.
* Continuously test ad variations, keywords, landing pages, and audiences for measurable lift.


Conversion Tracking \& Technical Expertise

* Set up and troubleshoot conversions through Google Tag Manager, Google Analytics 4, Google Ads conversion tracking, and server\-side tracking setups.
* Understand attribution models and how to interpret multi\-touch data.
* Diagnose tracking mismatches or data anomalies with confidence.
* Work effectively with developers when technical support is needed.


Client Management \& Communication

* Be the expert in the room \- able to explain complex concepts in a simple, confident, professional way.
* Present monthly performance reports with clarity, recommendations, and next steps.
* Translate data into actionable insights clients can understand and trust.
* Manage expectations, timelines, and strategic priorities with professionalism.


Performance Analysis

* Monitor budgets, pacing, and forecasts across all accounts.
* Conduct deep audits to identify underperforming areas and growth opportunities.
* Understand how Google Ads integrates with the full marketing funnel (SEO, CRO, email, creative).
* Build and maintain dashboards for clear reporting and trend analysis.


Collaboration

* Work side\-by\-side with creative and dev teams to align ads, landing pages, and tracking.
* Collaborate with internal PMs to manage tasks, deliverables, and deadlines.


Provide strategic recommendations to improve client results across all channels.
