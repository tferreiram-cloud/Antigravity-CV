**Job Summary** 



* Contribute to market coverage expansion and business maximization of TVs, Audio, and Monitors by developing a business plan, negotiating, and securing approvals with local and global stakeholders, followed by operationalizing the strategy on a day\-to\-day basis.
* Focus on delivering consumer value, driving sales growth, managing budgets, and optimizing product category profitability (PL) through the management of the product lifecycle, encompassing GTM processes, pricing, channel strategies, and point\-of\-sale (PDV) preparation.



  





  





**Opportunities to working** 
 with the VD team and cross\-functions and divisions.
 



  





  





**Areas of Responsibilities** 
 in this role, you will be responsible for:
 


* Overseeing the introduction of new product portfolios into the Brazilian market, coordinating all stages of the process.
* GTM Chrono Control à Rhythm / Alignments / FUP \& Feeding and specially PRE\-POST quality audit. HQ guideline’s track and debrief \- Ample visibility HQ NPI Plan \& schedule and drive to guide local GTM
* Product Deliverables à Category Positioning \- POD \& Hierarchy definitions aligned to local particularities
* Local \& Global \& Regulatory Handling / Investigation / Understanding such as Market, Trends, Competition
* Collaborating with various departments, from headquarters to local teams, to align on briefings, specifications, and product launch strategies.
* Driving and Monitoring campaigns, with clear definition of KPIs and specially budget control per line
* Conducting competitive analysis to define the optimal product portfolio.
* Developing and execute sales channel strategies, pricing, and promotional initiatives.
* Leading the training of sales and trade marketing teams, ensuring alignment on product features and strategic objectives.
* Supporting the development and implementation of communication campaigns, both digital and offline.
* Managing events and strategic partnerships for product and application launches.
* Quality Audit and check\-point to be fully aware of main issues, resolutions, cost Impacts
