Associate Product Manager/Product Manager (Retail) - ProEdge Services

Positions open for both APM \& PM
 



  





**Role Overview for APM** 




 We are looking for a motivated and curious individual from within our team to step into the
 



 role of Associate Product Manager. In this role, you will be the "right hand" to our Product
 



 Manager, helping to bridge the gap between high\-level strategy and day\-to\-day execution.
 



 Your primary mission is to accelerate our shipping speed by taking ownership of the
 



 discovery process, validating new ideas through testing, and ensuring the product backlog
 



 remains well\-structured, prioritized, and execution\-ready. This role is ideal for someone who
 



 is close to the business, curious about problem\-solving.
 



  





 The APM works cross\-functionally with Engineering, Design, and stakeholders to turn
 



 insights into tested solutions and help the team ship the right things efficiently.
 



  





**Key Responsibilities for APM** 




 Product Discovery \& Validation
 



 • Support product discovery efforts by helping identify business pain points and
 



 opportunities
 



 • Assist in defining hypotheses and success metrics for new features
 



 • Coordinate and help run experiments, prototypes, and feature tests
 



 • Conduct user research activities such as interviews, surveys, usability tests, and
 



 feedback analysis
 



 Backlog Management
 



 • Support the product manager in writing, refining, and maintaining clear user stories
 



 and acceptance criteria
 



 • Ensure backlog items are well\-prioritized, groomed, and ready for development
 



 • Collaborate with Engineering to answer questions, clarify requirements, and reduce
 



 ambiguity
 



 • Track progress and help manage dependencies and follow\-ups
 



 Product Delivery Support
 



  





 • Partner with the Product Manager to translate discovery insights into actionable
 



 product requirements
 



 • Support sprint planning, backlog refinement, and retrospectives
 



 • Help document product decisions, learnings, and outcomes
 



  





**What We’re Looking For In APM** 




 • English understanding and speaking is a must, since team is formed by people in
 



 Brazil and India
 



 • Strong analytical and problem\-solving skills
 



 • Ability to break down complex problems into clear, actionable tasks
 



 • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
 



 • Comfort working with cross\-functional teams
 



 • Organized, detail\-oriented, and able to manage multiple workstreams
