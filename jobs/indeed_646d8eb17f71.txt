**Job Summary**  

Provides business unit (Personal Lines, Claims, Commercial Lines) finance support by partnering with the business unit in developing and executing on business strategies and tactical operations to achieve overall company goals.

### **Essential Job Functions**


Provide business insight to the current financial situation of the business through analysis of key financial indicators on a regular basis. Works closely with the business unit to determine the risks and opportunities of the current financial climate and how best to mitigate risks and capitalize on opportunities. Identify financial opportunities and partner with business partner to develop, plan and execute on the opportunity with metrics and deliverables. Conduct competitor intelligence. Present complex ideas and concepts to business leaders and executives. Implement policies and procedures. Promotes safety at all times and complies with safety/ergonomic standards as outlined in relevant company published manuals.
