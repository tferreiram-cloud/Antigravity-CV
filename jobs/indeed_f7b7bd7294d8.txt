 **OVERVIEW:**


We are looking for a **Delivery Manager** to join our Interface team and act as a central execution and coordination hub across multiple initiatives and stakeholders.


This role is delivery\-focused, highly operational, and cross\-functional. You will not own product strategy, but you will play a key role in ensuring initiatives move forward smoothly, scopes and timelines are respected, and teams stay aligned in a fast\-paced, high\-demand environment.

**REQUIREMENTS:**

* 5\+ years of experience in roles such as: Delivery Manager, Project Manager, Scrum Master, Business Analyst, Systems Analyst
* Experience working in cross\-functional tech teams
* Previous technical exposure (e.g., systems analysis, development, or close engineering work) is a strong plus
* Comfortable working across multiple initiatives simultaneously
* Jira expertise is essential (ability to structure workflows, stories, epics, queries, and reports)
* Solid understanding of Agile methodologies (Scrum, Kanban)
* Strong experience with delivery tracking, execution, and reporting
* Excellent written and verbal communication
* Fluent English (you’ll lead and participate in international meetings)
* Strong organizational and prioritization skills

**PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS (Optional):**

* Experience with AI tools in daily workflows (e.g., Copilot, Cursor, similar)
* Familiarity with audit\-related reporting or time tracking controls
* Experience working with external vendors or partners

**WHAT YOU’LL DO:**

* Ensure product scopes, timelines, and delivery commitments are met
* Identify, anticipate, and remove impediments that may impact delivery
* Own delivery execution across multiple initiatives, ensuring things are moving forward
* Track and communicate status, risks, and dependencies clearly to stakeholders
* Be responsible for documentation and organization of initiatives, user stories, tasks and workflows in Jira
* Maintain accurate and up\-to\-date documentation of systems, services, and processes in Confluence
* Collaborate closely with Design, Engineering, and QA to deliver high\-quality solutions
* Follow initiatives end\-to\-end, from early discussions through launch, leading execution when needed
* Gather, clarify, and translate business and stakeholder requirements into delivery\-ready scopes
* Assess feasibility and flag risks early to leadership
* Ensure consistency, usability, and accessibility across components during delivery
* Communicate the product vision and roadmap for alignment purposes (without owning product strategy)
* Support and follow related projects, assisting team members when priorities overlap
* Support operational controls such as Jira hygiene, time tracking consistency, reporting for audits and leadership visibility
* Act as a key interface between engineering teams, PMs, Directors, external partners and vendors

  

This is a PJ contract based out of Brazil.
