CRM & Media Coordinator - Parfums Christian Dior

Develop, implement, and analyze Dior Beauty Brazil’s MEDIA and CRM strategies to drive brand awareness, performance, and customer engagement. Leverage advanced data analytics to optimize campaigns, enhance customer experiences, and achieve business objectives.
 



  





 Responsibilities:
 



 MEDIA
 


* Collaborate with the digital media agency to design and execute integrated digital campaigns, balancing brand awareness and performance\-driven e\-commerce goals.
* Strategically allocate the coop media budget across e\-retail partners to maximize ROI and drive sales performance.
* Ensure all off\-site communications align with Dior’s global brand guidelines and strategic priorities.
* Monitor and analyze campaign performance metrics to meet sell\-out targets, providing actionable insights.
* Oversee media budget tracking and ensure expenditures remain within allocated limits



 CRM
 


* Build and maintain a centralized customer database in Agenda Dior (or future platforms), integrating data from e\-commerce and perfumery channels.
* Develop and execute a customer marketing plan to enhance loyalty and retention.
* Identify and implement CRM opportunities within the e\-commerce ecosystem to elevate the customer journey.
* Partner with e\-retailer teams to develop a cohesive CRM strategy aligned with Dior’s brand vision.
* Identify CRM triggers and opportunities to enhance the Dior customer experience.
* Ensure seamless customer interactions with Dior across all retail touchpoints
* Analyze CRM campaign results to optimize engagement and conversions.

* Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Marketing, Economics, Engineering, or related fields.
* Collaborative team player with strong interpersonal skills.
* Passionate, proactive, and enthusiastic about luxury beauty and brand excellence.
* Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
