**About the Role**



We're looking for a driven, strategic, and sharp\-witted Account Manager to join the dynamic and growing sales team in São Paulo, Brazil! You will be the spokesperson for Topsort's cutting\-edge retail media APIs and products, working with the Top retailers, marketplaces, and delivery apps across Brazil to drive sales and growth in the market.


**Responsibilities**


* **Client Relationship Management:** Build and maintain strong, long\-term relationships with key clients in the **retail media sector**. Serve as the primary point of contact, ensuring client needs and expectations are met.
* **Onboarding \& Training:** Guide new clients through onboarding, ensuring they understand and effectively use the software. Provide ongoing training and support to maximize platform adoption.
* **Upselling \& Revenue Growth:** Analyze client needs to identify upsell opportunities. Develop and present proposals that demonstrate value, set and achieve revenue targets, and drive increased ad spend and account growth.
* **Strategic Advising:** Act as a **trusted advisor**, providing insights and recommendations to optimize clients' retail media campaigns. Stay updated on industry trends to offer value\-driven advice.
* **Issue Resolution:** Address client concerns proactively, collaborating with internal teams (data science, product, sales) to resolve issues efficiently.
* **Cross\-Functional Collaboration:** Work closely with **sales, marketing, product, and support** teams to ensure client success. Share best practices with the global account management team.


**Requirements**


* **Education \& Skills:** Bachelor's degree or MBA, preferably with a technical background. Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
* **Retail Media Expertise:** Proven experience in **account management** within **retail media or advertising**. Familiarity with relevant technologies.
* **Sales \& Strategy:** Ability to **identify and pursue upselling opportunities**, strategic thinking, and problem\-solving skills.
* **Entrepreneurial Mindset:** Independent and team\-oriented, with strong organizational and time management skills.
* **Technical Fluency:** Comfortable working with technical solutions and translating technical capabilities into retailer\-friendly language.
