Talent Acquisition Senior Manager - DLOCAL

**Why should you join dLocal?**
dLocal enables the biggest companies in the world to collect payments in 40 countries in emerging markets. Global brands rely on us to increase conversion rates and simplify payment expansion effortlessly. As both a payments processor and a merchant of record where we operate, we make it possible for our merchants to make inroads into the world’s fastest\-growing, emerging markets.  

By joining us you will be a part of an amazing global team that makes it all happen. Being a part of dLocal means working with 1000\+ teammates from 30\+ different nationalities and developing an international career that impacts millions of people’s daily lives. We are builders, we never run from a challenge, we are customer\-centric, and if this sounds like you, we know you will thrive in our team. **What’s the opportunity?**  

As our Talent Acquisition Senior Manager, you will lead the global strategy and end\-to\-end operations of Talent Acquisition at dLocal, across both tech and business functions. You will partner with Heads/VPs to plan headcount, calibrate profiles, and raise the bar on quality of hiring while ensuring predictable delivery. You’ll own the funnel from intake to offer, driving process simplification and global standardization. A core focus will be defining and executing the **automation and AI** agenda in TA to reduce manual work and bias and increase accuracy. Your impact will be measured through **Quality of Hire**, predictability, and operational efficiency.
### **What will you be doing?**

* Leading the global TA strategy and operations across tech and business roles, ensuring regional consistency and alignment with business priorities.
* Building, coaching, and scaling a high‑performing global TA team with clear goals, defined roles, and performance rituals.
* Establishing standards, **SLAs**, and governance from intake to offer, prioritizing quality over volume and improving predictability and time‑to‑offer.
* Partnering with **Heads/VPs** on workforce planning, profile calibration, and evidence‑based hiring decisions.
* Driving process simplification and standardization (intake, interviews, scorecards, approvals) to remove bottlenecks and rework at global scale.
* Defining and driving the **TA automation and AI** roadmap (ATS/CRM, AI‑assisted screening, programmatic outreach, scheduling, talent pools, reporting) to reduce manual work and bias and increase pipeline accuracy.
* Implementing a **Quality‑of‑Hire** and quality‑of‑process framework (criteria, scorecards, KPIs like stage conversion, time‑to‑offer, QoH, early turnover) and running continuous improvement cycles.
* Strengthening employer branding with Comms/Marketing and leadership to ensure consistent candidate experiences across regions and functions.

### **What skills do you need?**

* Proven experience leading **TA teams for technology and business** with global scope and direct partnership with VPs/C‑level.
* Demonstrated success simplifying and standardizing recruiting processes with measurable impact on cycle time and hiring quality.
* Hands‑on experience with **automation and AI** in the selection process (screening, scheduling, programmatic sourcing, analytics) and strong **ATS/CRM** ownership.
* Track record of shifting from **volume to quality**, defining and managing KPIs such as Quality of Hire, time‑to‑offer, and stage conversion.
* Global leadership: building and managing multicultural teams with performance rituals, coaching, and career development.
* Data‑driven, product mindset: translating funnel problems into scalable solutions, prioritizing a backlog, and measuring ROI for automation initiatives.
* **Advanced English (mandatory)**; Spanish is a plus.
